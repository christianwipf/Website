Christian Wipf

I am a fixed term lecturer in Economics at the University of Essex. I obtained my PhD at the University of Bern in 2020.

My research interests are in macroeconomics, monetary economics, banking and financial economics.

I have recently worked on payment systems, narrow banking and CBDC proposals, optimal inflation targets, and monetary policy transmission since the Great Financial Crisis.

Please see my cv and my resume for more details.
